About

I am an Integrated M.S.–Ph.D. student at Hanyang University (HYU NLP Lab). I study how large language models acquire and organize knowledge, and how we can reliably manipulate it post hoc. I focus on mechanistic interpretability, knowledge editing, unlearning, and multilingual safety.

Projects

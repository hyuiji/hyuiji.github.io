About Me

I am an Integrated M.S.–Ph.D. student at Hanyang University (HYU NLP Lab), advised by Prof. Taeuk Kim.

My research goal is to build robust and controllable language models. Currently, I focus on how LLMs acquire, organize, and utilize knowledge. My primary research interests lie in:

  • Mechanistic Interpretability: Understanding internal reasoning circuits.
  • Knowledge Editing & Unlearning: Reliably manipulating model beliefs.
  • Multilingual Safety: Ensuring safety across diverse languages.

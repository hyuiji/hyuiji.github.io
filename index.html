About Me

I am an Integrated M.S.–Ph.D. student at Hanyang University (HYU NLP Lab), advised by Prof. Taeuk Kim.

My research focus has recently expanded to Agent-based AI Systems and Embodied AI. I am interested in building autonomous agents that can plan, reason, and interact with complex environments.

  • Agent Frameworks: ReAct, Reflexion, and beyond.
  • Embodied AI: Bridging language models with action and perception.
  • Reliable NLP: Knowledge editing, unlearning, interpretability, and multilingual safety (Previous focus).
